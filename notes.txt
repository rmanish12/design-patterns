Design patterns are advanced object-oriented solution to commonly occuring software problems.

The 23 Gang of Four(GoF) patterns are generally considered the foundation for all other patterns.

They are categorized into three groups:
1. Creational
2. Structural
3. Behavioural

----------------------------------------------------------------------

Creational patterns:
1. Abstract Factory
2. Builder
3. Factory method
4. Prototype
5. Singleton


Structural patterns
1. Adapter
2. Bridge
3. Composite
4. Decorator
5. Facade
6. Flyweight
7. Proxy


Behavioural patterns
1. Chain of response
2. Command
3. Interpreter
4. Iterator
5. Mediator
6. Observer
7. Memento
8. State
9. Strategy
10. Template method
11. Visitor
